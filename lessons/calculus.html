Lesson for 14-16 Years Old

Welcome to Calculus! In this lesson, we will cover the basics of differentiation and integration.

Introduction to Derivatives

Learn about the concept of derivatives, including the definition and basic rules of differentiation.

Basic Integration

Understand the basics of integration and how it relates to finding areas under curves.

Applications

Explore simple applications of derivatives and integrals in real-life scenarios, such as calculating speed and area.

Lesson for 16-18 Years Old

In this lesson, we will delve deeper into calculus by exploring more advanced concepts and applications.

Advanced Differentiation

Learn about advanced differentiation techniques, including the product, quotient, and chain rules.

Advanced Integration

Explore advanced integration methods, such as integration by parts and substitution.

Applications

Understand how to apply calculus to solve more complex problems, such as optimization and finding volumes of solids of revolution.

Lesson for University Level

At the university level, calculus forms the foundation for more advanced mathematical and engineering concepts. Here we will explore some of these concepts.

Multivariable Calculus

Study the extension of calculus to functions of several variables. Understand partial derivatives, multiple integrals, and their applications.

Vector Calculus

Learn about vector fields, line integrals, and surface integrals. Explore important theorems such as Green's, Stokes', and the Divergence theorem.

Applications in Differential Equations

Understand the role of calculus in solving ordinary and partial differential equations, which are fundamental in various fields of science and engineering.
