Lesson for 14-16 Years Old

Welcome to Trigonometry! In this lesson, we will cover the basics of trigonometric ratios and their applications.

Basic Trigonometric Ratios

Learn about the basic trigonometric ratios: sine, cosine, and tangent. Understand how to calculate these ratios for different angles in a right triangle.

Using Trigonometric Ratios

Understand how to use trigonometric ratios to find missing sides and angles in right triangles.

Applications

Explore real-world applications of trigonometry, such as finding the height of a tree or a building using trigonometric ratios.

Lesson for 16-18 Years Old

In this lesson, we will delve deeper into trigonometry by exploring more advanced concepts and applications.

Trigonometric Identities

Learn about important trigonometric identities such as the Pythagorean identity and angle sum and difference identities.

Graphs of Trigonometric Functions

Explore the graphs of sine, cosine, and tangent functions. Understand their properties, such as amplitude, period, and phase shift.

Solving Trigonometric Equations

Understand how to solve trigonometric equations using algebraic methods and trigonometric identities.

Lesson for University Level

At the university level, trigonometry forms the foundation for more advanced mathematical concepts. Here we will explore some of these concepts.

Inverse Trigonometric Functions

Study the inverse trigonometric functions and their properties. Understand how to solve equations involving inverse trigonometric functions.

Trigonometric Integrals and Derivatives

Learn about the integration and differentiation of trigonometric functions. Understand how to apply these concepts to solve problems in calculus.

Complex Numbers and Euler's Formula

Explore the relationship between trigonometry and complex numbers. Understand Euler's formula and its applications in different areas of mathematics.
